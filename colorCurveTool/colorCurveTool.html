Curved HSV Palette Ramp Tool

Generate one continuous saturation/value curve from desaturated white to saturated black. The chosen base color pins the curve shape, and the ramp samples beads along that curve while shifting hue toward the highlights and shadows. Then derive warmer/lighter and cooler/darker companion ramps.
