Previously reported and now fixed:

- Screen sharing worked on localhost but failed remotely with "This
  browser cannot capture the screen."  Cause: browsers only allow
  getDisplayMedia over https, or over http to localhost.  Fix: optional
  HTTPS support via OpenSSL.

  Build with TLS:
      gcc -DUSE_TLS -o cscreen -lpthread -lssl -lcrypto cscreen.c

  Run with your certbot PEM files (both ports become TLS, WebSocket
  becomes wss):
      cscreen 5050 5051 \
        /etc/letsencrypt/live/YOURHOST/fullchain.pem \
        /etc/letsencrypt/live/YOURHOST/privkey.pem

  Reach it by the certificate's hostname, not by IP or localhost.  The
  plain (no-TLS) build still works unchanged for localhost testing.  See
  README.md, section "HTTPS", for details.
